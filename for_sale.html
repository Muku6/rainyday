For Sale

Sizing

The difference between a Men's and Women's shoe size used to be stated as 2 sizes difference. Rather recently I've been hearing and reading that the difference between them is 1½ sizes. It can also vary depending on the brand as well as material the shoe is made from. Don't take it as a hard and fast rule. Width of your foot can play a role in this all as well. What is displayed is what is shown in shoe, for your convenience. (For example, a Men's size 10 shoe would be equivilant of a Woman's size 12. On the flip side a Woman's size 10 shoe would be a Men's size 8.)

Tax

If you live in the state of Washington, tax will be applied on PayPal accordingly. Rainy Day Art is based in Washington state and therefore are required by state law to collect taxes from those living within the state.

Shipping

Shipping is done via Priority, with insurance, is included in all of the prices listed for those living within the contiguous United States. If you live elsewhere, such as Hawai'i, Alaska, or in another country, and are interested in a pair, please don't purchase through the Buy Now button. You will need to contact me as soon as possible so that I can set aside the only pair. There are only one pair available of each. Make sure to note where you live and the name of the pair of shoes you are interested in. This will allow me to calculate a shipping cost for you. Do note that any customs fee incurred will fall upon the buyer. Cost does not include possible customs fees upon entering another country.
